ABOUT

Presidentl is a daily puzzle about the 47 presidencies of the United States. Each day at midnight a new president is chosen in secret and you have six guesses. Every guess reveals how close you stand on five measures: their number, how many vice presidents served under them, the year they were sworn in, their party, and how many terms they held. Green is exact, gold is close, and arrows point the way. The Pub Quiz runs endlessly alongside it.

Who makes it

Presidentl is built and maintained by Keystone Dynamic, a small studio making daily quiz games. We also make Orbil, the daily globe game.
