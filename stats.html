YOUR STATS

Every puzzle, every streak, tallied.

Your current streak, best streak, win rate and guess distribution — how often you've solved it in one guess, two, three, and so on.

All of it lives in your browser's local storage, on your own device. There's no account and no sign-up; we never see your record and can't read it. Clearing your browser data erases it for good, and playing on another device starts a fresh record.

Your streak grows by one for each consecutive day you solve the puzzle, and resets if you miss a day or run out of guesses. Back to today's puzzle.
