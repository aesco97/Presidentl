PRESIDENTL

One puzzle · every day · no runoffs.

Presidentl is a daily puzzle about the 47 presidencies of the United States. Each day at midnight a new president is chosen in secret, and everyone gets the same puzzle. You have six guesses. Every guess reveals how close you stand on five measures: their number in order, how many vice presidents served under them, the year they were sworn in, their party, and how many terms they held. Green is exact, gold is close, and arrows point you toward the answer.

Play today's puzzle or try the Pub Quiz — endless practice across every category, at easy, medium or hard difficulty.

Free, no account, a new puzzle every midnight.
