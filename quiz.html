PUB QUIZ

Endless practice. No runoffs.

Pick a difficulty, pick a mode, and go. Three ways to learn the 47 presidencies:

Learning — the gentlest. Every answer is revealed as you go, and anything you miss comes back a few questions later, and again, until you have it. Drill the order of presidents or mixed trivia.
Name Them All — all 47 in order, from Washington to today.
Trivia — a mix of vice presidents, events, terms, portraits and chronology. Choose ten questions, twenty, or endless, and switch categories on and off.

Four difficulties, and they change more than the scoring:

Easy — three options drawn from far-flung eras; told the moment you're wrong, with three tries at each president.
Medium — four options from the same neighbourhood of history, and one retry each.
Hard — six options from the same era and party, one attempt, and you learn nothing until the end.
Extra Hard — as Hard, but no names are suggested at all. You spell all 47 yourself, from memory, and find out how you did only when it's over.

Trivia runs from straightforward to roughly AP US History standard. Or come back to today's puzzle.
